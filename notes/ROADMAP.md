# Project Roadmap

Working with **Counter:Side** involves a massive amount of "under-the-hood" effort—decryption and library management often represent the bulk of the work before a single game element is even visible. I am **not** aiming for a 1:1 replication of the in-game experience.

### Current Focus

My primary goal is to polish the core experience of the **basic story reader**.
* Improving non-English language support and usability. Non-English speaking communities were a huge part of the Counter:Side experience after all.
* Fixing immersion-breaking bugs.

### Future
* Open Source: Once the reader reaches a "Good Enough" state, I'll release a separate repository with source files for the community to expand upon.
